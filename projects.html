Master's Thesis, IIT Kanpur (May’15- Ongoing)

Rough Terrain Path Planning for a Biped Robot
Under the guidance of Prof. Ashish Dutta, IIT Kanpur
The objective was to a develop a footstep plan on a rough terrain for a 12DOF biped robot. Kinematic and state transition model of biped for walking and turning over uneven terrain was developed and inverse dynamics was performedusing Euler-Lagrange approach. Energy minimization using GA was performed for a variety of turning angles, step-lengths and slopes to generate a database. Database was utlized to train two Neural Networks- for real-time pattern generation and energy estimation. Finally, an A* search to develop step-plan based on energy minimization on obstacle filled rough surface.

UG Research Project, IIT Kanpur (Oct’14-May’15)

Investigating the Self-Sensing Capabilities of Piezopatch in a Composite Laminate
A mathematical framework for evaluating the self-sensing capabilities of a piezoelectric patch. The system comprises of a composite material with piezopatch embedded at a given location. The piezo is energized via an input voltage which in turn induces strain in the piezo i.e. at this stage piezo acts as an induced strain actuator. However, due to its interaction with the composite material stress is generated in the entire structure. This stress further induces a voltage, thus harnessing the self-sensing capabilities of the piezo. A full length paper presented at ISS NC7 organized by IISc Banagalore is available...here

Genetic Algorithm for Combinatorial Optimization of Weight of a Composite Plate

Maximum allowable displacement for given loading condition was taken as the design constraint.
